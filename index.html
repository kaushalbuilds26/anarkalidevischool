About Us

Building Bright Futures in Rural U.P.

Smt. Anarkali Devi Higher Secondary School was established in 2006 in the village of Firozpur Siloni, Marhara Block, Etah district. As a private unaided institution, the school has been a beacon of quality education for the rural community for nearly two decades.

We follow the Uttar Pradesh State Board curriculum and provide Hindi-medium instruction in a welcoming co-educational environment. With dedicated teachers and computer-aided learning tools, we prepare students from Nursery through Class 10 for a bright future.
